Frequently Asked Questions

What blockchain networks does Klerno Labs support?
Klerno Labs currently supports major blockchain networks including Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP Ledger, Binance Smart Chain, and Polygon. We're continuously adding support for additional networks based on user demand and market requirements.
How accurate are the risk scores provided by the platform?
Our risk scoring algorithm achieves over 95% accuracy in identifying high-risk transactions. The scores are based on machine learning models trained on millions of transactions and are continuously updated to reflect new threat patterns and regulatory requirements.
Can I integrate Klerno Labs with my existing compliance systems?
Yes, Klerno Labs provides comprehensive API integration capabilities. You can connect our platform to your existing AML, KYC, and compliance management systems using our RESTful APIs and webhook notifications.
What data retention policies does Klerno Labs follow?
We retain transaction data for up to 7 years to comply with financial regulations. Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for service provision. You can request data deletion at any time, subject to legal requirements.
How does Klerno Labs ensure data privacy and security?
We implement bank-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, regular security audits, and multi-factor authentication. All data is stored in secure, geographically distributed data centers with 24/7 monitoring.
